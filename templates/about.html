About Notes App

A simple and secure platform to manage your personal notes anytime and anywhere.

The Notes Management System is built using Flask, MySQL, and Bootstrap to provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly note-taking experience.

Users can create, edit, update, and delete notes securely, helping them organize ideas, tasks, and important information in one convenient place.

⚡ Fast

Quickly create and manage notes with ease.

📱 Responsive

Works smoothly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
